Why Decent?

Here you can find some Decent benefits:

It’s Ethical

We don't track your online behaviours, we won’t show you ads or promoted tweets.

We’ll respect you and your freedom.

It’s Privacy-first

We are using Nitter behind the scenes.
No Twitter account required.
No collection of personal data.

We care about you, not your personal data.

It’s Lightweight & clean

Fast and distraction-free Twitter experience
10x smaller script
Mobile First, optimized for the Web

It’s Open-Source

Yes! Decent is open-source on GitHub
You can opt for self-hosting Decent

You are free to contribute!
